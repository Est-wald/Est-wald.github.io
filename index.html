Mathematical interest:

  • I am interested in infinite-dimensional mathematics. For instance, chiral algebras (algebra), jet schemes (geometry). These objects, together with various avatars, have shown up a lot in quantum field theory and string theory.
  • I am interested in the geometric Langlands program which is again closely related to physics. ♣
  • I am interested in derived algebraic geometry because I believe that it will make life simpler and will become a basic tool in geometry and representation theory.

♣ There is a famous book Love and Math by Ed Frenkel. Accordingly, Vanya Karpov once made an assertion: "Some mathematicians work for love while some work for the Langlands program."
